"Naalukettu"

A classic novel by one of the most prominent Malayalam writers, M.T. Vasudevan Nair. It explores the changing social and cultural landscape of Kerala through the story of a traditional Nair family.

"Pride and Prejudice"

This classic novel is a romantic comedy about the manners and matrimonial machinations among the British gentry of the early 19th century. The story revolves around Elizabeth Bennet and her complex relationship with the wealthy and aloof Mr. Darcy.

"To Kill a Mockingbird"

This Pulitzer Prize-winning novel is set in the American South during the 1930s and addresses serious issues like racial injustice and moral growth. "Klara and the Sun"

A science fiction novel by the Nobel Prize-winning author, it is told from the perspective of Klara, an Artificial Friend (a robot companion) who observes the complexities of human emotions and relationships. The book explores themes of love, loneliness, and the ethical implications of artificial intelligence.
